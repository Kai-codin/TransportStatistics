# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
