Import Your Transit Tracker Data

Welcome! To get started, you can import your Transit tracker data into your account.

How to Import Data
  1. Get your data:

    Email gdpr@transittracker.net and request a copy of your data.
    This normally takes a couple hours.

  2. Once you have your data

    Unzip the file they send you and find your services.json file.

  3. Import your data

    Once you have your services.json file, go to Settings and upload the services.json file.

  4. Click Upload and scan to start the import process.

    Then select your import option. (Skip dupes, Overwrite dupes, Import all)

  5. And you're done!
